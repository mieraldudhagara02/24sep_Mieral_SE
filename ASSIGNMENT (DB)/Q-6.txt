Q-6. What is DDL Interpreter?

-->  SQL statements are divided into two major categories:
        1.DDL
        2.DML

- DDL stands for Data Definition Language.
- DML stands for Data Manipulation Language.

==>  DDL:-

-->  Data Definition Language is a subset of SQL.

-->  DDL statements are used to build and modify the structure of your
     tables and other objects in the database.

-->  When you execute a DDL statement, it makes effect immediately.

-->  SQL DDL commands are further divided into the following major categories:

         * CREATE
         * ALTER
         * DROP
         * TRUNCATE
